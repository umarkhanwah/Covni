COVID-19 Symptoms

Fever

High body temperature, often accompanied by chills.

Cough

A dry or productive cough, sometimes with mucus.

Shortness of Breath

Difficulty breathing, especially when active.

Fatigue

Feeling extremely tired or lethargic.

Loss of Taste or Smell

Not being able to taste or smell things.
